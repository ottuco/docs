# Payment Services

Payment services are digital wallet and mobile payment methods that let your customers pay using their devices — phones, tablets, and laptops — instead of entering card details manually. These services offer faster checkout, stronger security through device-level authentication (biometrics, PINs), and a smoother customer experience.

Ottu supports the following payment services:

| Service | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **[Apple Pay](apple-pay/)** | Accept payments from customers using iPhone, iPad, Mac, and Apple Watch. Supported in Kuwait, Saudi Arabia, and Bahrain. |
| **[Samsung Pay](samsung-pay)** | Accept payments from customers using Samsung devices and Samsung Wallet. |

Each payment service requires a one-time setup process involving the service provider's developer portal and your Ottu dashboard. The guides below walk you through every step.

## What's Next?

- **[Apple Pay](apple-pay/)** — Set up Apple Pay with merchant ID, domain verification, and certificates
- **[Samsung Pay](samsung-pay)** — Register with Samsung and link your Service ID to Ottu
- **[Payment Gateways](/docs/business/payments/gateways)** — Browse and configure your payment gateways
- **[Payment Routing](/docs/business/payments/routing)** — Route payments to the best gateway automatically
