# Tenant & Contract Management

This section provides merchants with key details about managing tenants and contracts in the Ottu Real Estate Plugin.

## What is a Tenant?

A **tenant** is an individual or entity renting property from a landlord. Tenants sign a **contract** that specifies:

- **Rent payment** terms
- **Tenancy duration**
- **Responsibilities** of both parties

Tenants may rent **residential**, **commercial**, or **industrial** properties, depending on their needs. The plugin supports both individual tenants and company tenants (Limited Liability Company or Listed Company).

## What is a Contract?

A **contract** is a legally binding agreement outlining the rights and responsibilities of the landlord and tenant. It includes:

- **Rent amount**
- **Tenancy duration**
- **Rules and responsibilities**

Contracts provide legal protection and help prevent disputes by clearly defining expectations for both parties.
