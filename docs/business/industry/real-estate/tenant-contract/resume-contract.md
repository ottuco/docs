# Resume Contract

When a [suspended contract](/docs/business/industry/real-estate/tenant-contract/suspend-contract) is resumed, all transactions related to the invoices associated with this contract become payable again. Tenants can once again make payments against these invoices, and the contract returns to its active status.

## How to Resume a Contract

To resume a suspended contract, follow the steps described in [Triggering Contract Actions](/docs/business/industry/real-estate/tenant-contract/contract-management#triggering-contract-actions).

Select **Resume Contract** from the action menu to reactivate the contract immediately.
