# Terminate Contract

Ottu enables merchants to efficiently terminate contracts when a tenancy ends. To initiate a contract termination, follow the steps described in [Triggering Contract Actions](/business/industry/real-estate/tenant-contract/contract-management#triggering-contract-actions).

## Termination Process

Upon selecting **Terminate Contract**, you are directed to the termination page where you complete the process by entering:

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| **Termination Date** | The official date the contract is terminated |
| **Actual Leave Date** | The date the tenant actually vacates the property |
| **Leave Notice Date** | The date the tenant provided notice of leaving |

After filling in these fields, click **Save** and then confirm to finalize the termination.

![Terminate contract form](/img/business/real-estate/placeholder.png)

:::warning
Terminating a contract is a significant action. Make sure to verify the termination details before confirming. If a termination was made in error, it can be rolled back from the [Auditing](/business/industry/real-estate/auditing#terminate-contract-list) section.
:::
