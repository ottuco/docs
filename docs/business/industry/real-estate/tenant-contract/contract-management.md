# Contract Management

The Ottu Real Estate Plugin enables merchants to efficiently manage property rental agreements. It offers tools to create, renew, modify, and terminate contracts while tracking key details such as status, dates, and payments. This ensures seamless management and clear oversight of all contractual obligations.

## Contract Lifecycle

A contract in the Real Estate Plugin goes through several stages:

1. **Creation** -- A new contract is [created](/business/industry/real-estate/tenant-contract/add-contract) linking a tenant to a property unit with defined terms.
2. **Active** -- The contract is in force; invoices are generated and payments collected.
3. **Renewal** -- An active contract can be [renewed](/business/industry/real-estate/tenant-contract/renew-contract) with updated terms and a new end date.
4. **Suspension** -- A contract can be [suspended](/business/industry/real-estate/tenant-contract/suspend-contract), halting all invoice payments.
5. **Resumption** -- A suspended contract can be [resumed](/business/industry/real-estate/tenant-contract/resume-contract), reactivating payments.
6. **Termination** -- A contract can be [terminated](/business/industry/real-estate/tenant-contract/terminate-contract) when the tenancy ends.

## Triggering Contract Actions

Merchants can perform contract actions from the [Tenant & Contract Dashboard](/business/industry/real-estate/tenant-contract/dashboard):

1. Go to the **Tenant and Contract Management** section.
2. Search for the tenant and property associated with the contract you want to manage.
3. At the end of the contract's row, click on the **three dots** (**...**).

![Contract action menu](/img/business/real-estate/placeholder.png)

The following actions are available:

| Action | Description |
|---|---|
| [Renew Contract](/business/industry/real-estate/tenant-contract/renew-contract) | Extend an existing contract with a new end date and optionally updated amounts |
| [Terminate Contract](/business/industry/real-estate/tenant-contract/terminate-contract) | End a contract by specifying termination and leave dates |
| [Suspend Contract](/business/industry/real-estate/tenant-contract/suspend-contract) | Temporarily block all invoice payments for a contract |
| [Resume Contract](/business/industry/real-estate/tenant-contract/resume-contract) | Reactivate a previously suspended contract |
| [Manual Payment](/business/industry/real-estate/tenant-contract/manual-payment) | Record an offline payment against a contract's invoices |
| [Advance Payment](/business/industry/real-estate/tenant-contract/advance-payment) | Request and process advance rent payments from a tenant |
