# Payment Management

Ottu gives you full visibility into every payment that flows through your account. From searching and filtering individual transactions to monitoring overall sales performance, everything you need to track and manage payments lives in the dashboard.

This section covers four areas:

## Search & Filter Payments

Find any payment quickly using filters for user, payment gateway, date range, and more. Customize which columns appear in your transaction table so the data you care about is always visible.

[Go to Search & Filter](/docs/business/payment-management/search-and-filter)

## Transaction Insights

Understand your transaction data through the transaction table and dashboard charts. Review amounts, see breakdowns of settled vs. paid vs. refunded amounts, and monitor business performance with visual charts.

[Go to Transaction Insights](/docs/business/payment-management/transaction-insights)

## Transaction States

Every payment moves through a series of states from creation to completion. Understand what each state means, how parent and child transactions relate, and what happens when a customer retries a failed payment.

[Go to Transaction States](/docs/business/payment-management/transaction-states)

## Notifications, URLs & Timing

Configure when and how notifications are sent, use short URLs for SMS delivery, and control how long payment links stay active before they expire.

[Go to Notifications & Timing](/docs/business/payment-management/notifications-timing)
